How do I get into the school?

During the first days, you will have to go to building 6 (the library) to get your ID card that will allow access to the school.

The third floor, which is called Learning Commons, is where you have to go to in order to acquire it. You will be taken a photo and you will have your card in no time.

This isn't the official one though. You will later in the semester receive an e-mail to pick up the ID card you will keep for the rest of your stay at Tec

Where is my classroom?

Refer to the image on the left. As you may see, there are 18 places to go to. Each building is labeled with a number that is seen on the top of them.

Your classroom number might be something like this: 6102

Where is it supposed to be? The first digit(s) represent the building number. The next digit represent the floor number and the last digit represents the classroom number.

So if your classroom number is 6102, that means you have to go to building 6 (the library), on the first floor, classroom number 2.

Where do I consult my schedule?

MiTec is the page to go.

This is one of ITESM's websites where you can consult your schedule, your teachers' schedules and your grades.

There are also other sections which contain a lot of information that you might find really helpful during your stay at Tec Campus Querétaro. For example, there is a section for obtaining software that's been licensed by the school, and more specific information may be found at MiTec as well.
