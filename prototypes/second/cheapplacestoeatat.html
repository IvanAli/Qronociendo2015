Cheap places to eat at

Low budget and hungry? Check out our recommendations

Biznaga

A bohemian small place at the center. Cheap and delicious mexican food, served on tipical mexican plate by hippies.

Disadvantages: It is only open mornings from 8am to 2pm and nights from 7pm to 11pm

Tip: Milkshakes are great!

Price range: $30.00 - $80.00


Address: Manuel Gutierrez Nájera 17 sur, Queretaro

La Central

Also mexican food, from tacos to pozole on the same place. The advantage from La Central it’s you can go by walk from tec, located right infront of prepa Tec Entrance.

Price range: $50.00 - $120.00

Disadvantages: Bambata changes a lot on friday, don’t spect the same people and ambiance, better just go on Thrusday

Address: Prolongación Tecnológico Norte 950 Local A2, Querétaro

Red Wings

If you are looking for good Chicken wings, this is the place. Located on the same mall as la Central, is the nearest place for wings.

Tip: on Tuesdays it's the wings buffet all the Chicken wings you can eat for $115

Price range: $75.00 - $200.00

Address: Prolongación Tecnológico #950 Plaza Mall, Querétaro
