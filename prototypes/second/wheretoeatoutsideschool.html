Where to eat outside school?

The places close to the campus where you can enjoy really nice dishes

Here in Tecnológico de Monterrey, academic schedules are so variable, you can have a class at 7 in the morning, then free time form 9:00 and then have to come back to class at 10:00! And it gets crazy when you does not have enough time to go back home and get some food, so here we give you some recommendations of places really near to the school, where you can get some cheap food and get a little bit of fresh air out of the school!
