Need a place to live at? We list where you can find the right place

We know coming to México without a house to live in can be tough, so we suggest two main things:

Don’t be afraid to ask people at Tec if they know any place to live in.

Look on Facebook, here we provide the links for two trustable groups to look for a roomie.

Tip: Don’t think cheap is better han nice, mostly the nice places have security or are in a safer zone, so think it’s better to spend a few bucks more than being on a situation where you can lose something you can’t recover.
