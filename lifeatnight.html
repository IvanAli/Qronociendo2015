Social life at night

Nice places to hang out at during nights

Rhodesia

This night club has just opened last March, and it is already one of the most popular clubs in Querétaro. If you like hipsters an rave music, this is the place you are looping for, there’s a diferent DJ each day, i’ts open from wendsay to saturday from 10 pm-3 am.

Disadvantages: a cover of $100 for men fridays and saturdays

Tips: Fridays and Saturdays are the best days to go.

You can check their schedule on Facebook.


Address: Av. 5 de Mayo no. 58 Col. Centro, Querétaro

Bambaata and Sirilo

These two clubs are right next to each other, so Qronociendo suggests you to know both of them.

Sirilo has a most hipster style and bambata goes more for hip hop. But what's great about this places is that if you show an ID from a diferent country, you got free shots from 11 pm-12 am every Thursday!

Disadvantages: Bambata changes a lot on friday, don’t spect the same people and ambiance, better just go on Thrusday

You can check Sirilo's Facebook page here and Bambata's official website here.

Address: Circuito Jardín S 1, Alamos 3ra Secc, 76160 Queretaro, QRO, Mexico, Queretaro

Manceba

Located at Querétaro's historic downtown, Manceba has a promotion everyday! For example Tuesdays you get 2x1 on mezcal and burritos.

If you are planning on going to Rhodesia on Thursday, you better stop at Manceba for 2 margaritas for just $60.

Tip: Don’t drink more than 5 mezcales if you don't want to wake up with a horrible hangover.

Address: Luis Pasteur 10 Centro, Querétaro
