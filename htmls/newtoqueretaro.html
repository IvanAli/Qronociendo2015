Bus routes

The most critical aspect when you're new to a city is getting to know where you are and how to get somewhere. You might not have a car, or can't afford a taxi, so you will need to know the bus routes as soon as possible.

Fortunately, there are some websites and some mobile apps which will be a great help to allow you to move around the city:

Ruta Directa Qro is a website that has all the list of routes that move around Querétaro. Not only that, but it has functionality to know what route to take from one point to another.

Are you an iPad/iPhone user? QRutas is the must-have app! Easy to use and you can filter routes as well.

Are you an Android phone user? Rutas Querétaro is just like Ruta Directa for your phone.
