Where to eat inside school?

The places inside the campus where you can fuel your stomach

La Terraza

Schedule: 7 am - 6 pm

It is located near Building 4 (the one with the font!) it has three sections:

One with fast food (hamburgers, French fries, tortas, quesadillas) around $50 - $80

Another section is a mini “tiendita” that offers cookies, chips, soda, water, candy, etc.

And the other section has the daily menu, different every day; one for breakfast that includes fruit, juice, coffee and a stew; and one for lunch that includes; soup, salad, stew, dessert, as much bread as you want and a refill flavored water for the price of $43.

La Café

Schedule: 7 am - 8:30 pm (Monday - Friday)

It is just behind “La Terraza” in Building

It’s a relaxing place, where some people get a coffee while doing homework, or get a snack during the day (credit cards accepted).

It offers: coffee either hot or cold, tea, sodas, smoothies, bagels, doughnuts, salads and more.

Prices go around $20.00 to $40.00

Want to feel refrehsed during the day? The chai tea is recommended!

Las ensaladas

It is a mini place where you can order healthy food, it is open from 7:00 am to 19:00 pm.

You can get fruit, juices, salads or sandwiches.

The system for getting a salad consists in 5 easy steps:

Get in line

Choose between the 3 sizes of salad: Small $42.00, Medium $53.00 or Big $68.00

Pay

Fill in a form with the ingredients you want

Give your order to the lady cooking and wait in line for your salad

Food station

Schedule: 8:00 am - 9 pm

This one is located in Building 15 (the one where all the industrial designers are all the time!)

It is the cheapest inside the school and the specialty is the sushi or the gohan!

Be sure to get your food early because during the night most of what they offer is already sold out.

La plaza café

This one is located in the cyber plaza by the “Borrego cósmico” (the statue of the cosmic sheep)

This one is a station that offers coffee, some Mexican food such as tacos dorados, sincronizada, tortas, molletes, burritos, etc, it also has some candy, pizza, sushi, French fries, fruit , juice and some desserts.

The price range is $10.00 - $40.00

Green Coffee Shop

This one is located in the “Learning Commons” best known as “Ruido” It is in the third floor of the library.

Here is where must of us go to study or doing team projects, specially during final exams; this place gets crazy!

People also hang out, play some video games or watch movies.

You can make noise and eat at the same time!

The Green Coffee shop offers you; bagels, salads, coffee, smoothies, chips, cookies, juice, water.

The price range is $15.00 - $40.00
