Traveling outside the city

Get to know what's around the city of Querétaro

First of all, we recommend to never travel alone; gather with some friends, either mexicans or foreign students.

Now the cities you must get to know are:



Cancún

The city is located on the Caribbean Sea, and is one of the easternmost points in Mexico. Cancún is located just north of Mexico's Caribbean coast resort band known as the Riviera Maya.

Ciudad de México

This is the capital of Mexico and seat of the federal powers of the union. It is a federal entity within Mexico which is not part of any one of the 31 Mexican states but belongs to the federation as a whole. Mexico City is the country's largest city as well as its most important political, cultural, educational and financial center.


San Miguel de Allende

The main attraction of the town is its well-preserved historic center, filled with buildings from the 17th and 18th centuries. This and the nearby Sanctuary of Atotonilco have been declared World Heritage Sites.

It’s a small town full of art with a great night life just 30 minutes away from Querétaro.
