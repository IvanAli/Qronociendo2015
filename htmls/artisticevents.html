Artistic events

Trending events and places related to art

Querétaro is a place full of culture. If you head downtown you could find 5 museums, 2 of the main art galeries and much more.

Here are our top recommendations of places and events:


Museo de la Ciudad

Click here to visit the website

Here the variety ranges from movies to sculptures, it has 3 exhibition rooms which change every month and a small theater with three completely different functions each day.

Museo de arte

Click here to visit the website

With 5 exhibit rooms, Museo de arte de Querétaro shows diferent expositions every month and also has a established one about Colonial Art. This museum is also handycap friendly and has an exposition for deaft and blind people each month.

Important piece of advice: Take your Tec ID so you won’t be charged to get in.

Cine teatro Rosalio Solano

Click here to visit the website

This movie teather shows Indie and foreing (not Hollywood) Films, every month is a diferent programing.



DocumentaQro

Click here to visit the website

Shows documentaries on public places, like Plaza de Armas and Galería Libertad, every Tuesday at 8 pm.

Galería Libertad

Click here to visit the website

This is the place where you can find modern art and Indie expositions that change every 2 weeks or every month.
